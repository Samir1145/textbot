Notes :

The application must be made within sixty days of the date upon which the applicant is served with the order of the Appellate Tribunal, which gives rise to the reference.
The application must be accompanied by a fee specified below :-
(a)	in a case where the assessment proceedings were initiated before the 1st day of April 1971, Rs. 100:
(b)	in a case where the assessment proceedings were initiated after the 31st day of March 1971, but before the 1st day of June, 1981, Rs. 125;
(c)	in any case, Rs. 200.

For the purpose of this Note, the assessment proceedings shall be deemed to have been initiated on either of the dates referred to in paragraph 6, whichever is earlier, except where the applicant is the Chief Commissioner of Commissioner. It is suggested that the fee should be credited in a branch of the authorised Bank or a branch of the State Bank of India or a branch of the Reserve Bank of India, after obtaining a challan from the Assessing Officer. The Tribunal will not accept cheques, drafts, hundies, or other negotiable instruments.
