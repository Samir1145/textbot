FILING OF FRESH SUIT IN RESPECT OF SAME SUBJECT MATTER WITHOUT LEAVE OF COURT
Order 23, Rules 1(3) and (4) and Order 2, Rule 2
Under sub-rule (4) of Order 23, Rule 1, when plaintiff withdraws from the suit without permission to file fresh suit for the same subject matter as contemplated under sub-rule (3) then such plaintiff is precluded from filing any fresh suit in respect of the same subject matter.1
1. Smt. Nirwala v. Hari Singh, AIR 2001 Himachal Pradesh.
