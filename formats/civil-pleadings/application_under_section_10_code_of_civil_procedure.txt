CASE LAW
Section 10
The ejectment proceedings and probate proceedings are distinct and different. Issue involved in two proceedings cannot be identical. There is no question of applicability of Section 10 C. P. C.1
STAY OF SUIT.
When the causes of action are entirely different and there is no common issue in both the suits prayer for staying later suit cannot be allowed.2
STAY OF SUIT FOR RECOVERY OF AMOUNT COVERED UNDER THE DISHONOURED CHEQUE
Sections 10 and 15
It is well settled that a suit filed for recovery of amount covered under the dishonoured cheques should not be stayed under Section 10 of the Code of Civil Procedure solely on the ground that Criminal Proceedings under Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act has been instituted.2A
