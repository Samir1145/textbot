CASE LAW
The teachers of an educational institution cannot be brought within the purview of the Minimum Wages Act and the State Government in exercise of powers under the Act is not entitled to fix the minimum wage of such teachers.1
1. Haryana Unrecognised Schools Association v. State of Haryana, 1996 (2) C. C. C. 158 (S. C.).
