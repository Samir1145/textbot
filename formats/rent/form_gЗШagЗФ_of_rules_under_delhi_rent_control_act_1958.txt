CASE LAW 
The term ‘Landlord’ in Section 21 of the Delhi Rent Control Act, 1958 cannot be understood in a different sense than that in Section 2(e) of the Act.1
Where a landlord inducts a tenant for a limited period after obtaining permission of the controller and he dies before expiry of the period of tenancy the legal representative of the landlord can initiate and continue proceeding for recovery of possession under Section 21 of the Act.2
