PROCEEDINGS FOR GRANT OF SUCCESSION CERTIFICATE
The proceedings for grant of succession certificate are summary in nature and that no rights are findly decided in such proceedings.1
Expression "claiming to have any interest in the estate of deceased" occurring in Section 283(1 )c of Indian Succession Act would mean any interest, however slight and even bare possibility of an interest would be sufficient to entitle a person to enter caveat and contest probate proceedings.2
